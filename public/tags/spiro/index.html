How the Provinces Conquer

Below is a translation of a lecture given by György Spiró in 2006. Here is the original hungarian transcript and videos here and here. The lecture explores how a superpower decays and falls due to the spread of ideas from its periphery. Nearly twenty years have passed since this lecture, and many parallels can be drawn with the present state of the world, but it isn’t always obvious what to consider the periphery or the actual center.
